A Journey Through Coastal Zones

NORTHERN BEACHES

Usually cooler climate, surrounded by hills or cliffs, often less crowded.
Serene landscapes, adventure sports like paragliding, cliff diving,
and peaceful sunsets.

POPULAR ACTIVITIES: rekking along coastal cliffs, photography, quiet relaxation.

SOUTHERN BEACHES

Tropical, warm waters, long stretches of golden sand, often lined with palm trees.

ATTRACTIONS: Vibrant marine life, water sports, and lively beach towns.

POPULAR ACTIVITIES: Surfing, snorkeling, beach parties, sunset cruises.

EASTERN BEACHES

Scenic coastal regions with coral reefs, often culturally rich areas with nearby historical sites.

POPULAR ACTIVITIES: Scuba diving, snorkeling, and exploring coastal villages.

WESTERN BEACHES

Dramatic cliffs, sandy shores, and often calmer waters, with a mix of tourist and untouched areas.

POPULAR ACTIVITIES: Sunset walks, fishing, and water sports.
